Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Cheese Asteroid Eater ("we", "our", or "us") is built to be a safe and fun experience. This policy explains how we handle data.

1. Game Center

Our game uses Apple Game Center to provide leaderboards and achievements. This data is handled by Apple. We do not store your personal Game Center information on our own servers.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personal identifiable information. Local high scores and settings (sound, music, vibration) are stored locally on your device via UserDefaults.

3. Third-Party Advertising

The game may display advertisements. These providers may use identifiers (IDFA) to show relevant ads. You can opt-out of tracking through your iOS system settings.

4. Children's Privacy

Our game is designed for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from children.

5. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us through the official support channels.
