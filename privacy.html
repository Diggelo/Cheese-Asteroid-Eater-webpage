Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Cheese Asteroid Eater ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to providing a safe and fun experience. This policy explains how we handle your data when you play our game.

1. Local Data Storage

We store your high scores and game settings (such as sound, music, and vibration preferences) locally on your device. This data stays on your phone and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Apple Game Center

Our game integrates with Apple Game Center to provide global leaderboards. When you are logged in, Apple may collect data such as your player ID and scores. This information is handled exclusively by Apple according to their privacy policy.

3. In-App Purchases

If you choose to purchase "Remove Ads", the transaction is processed securely through Apple's StoreKit. We do not have access to your credit card information or financial details. We only receive a confirmation that the purchase was successful to unlock the feature.

4. Advertising and Tracking

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. To provide a better experience, we use Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework to ask for your permission to track activity.

  • Identifiers: If you opt-in, AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to show personalized ads.
  • Usage Data: AdMob may collect information about how you interact with ads (taps and views).
  • Diagnostics: We may collect anonymized crash logs and performance data to improve the game's stability.

You can change your tracking preferences at any time in your iOS System Settings under "Privacy & Security > Tracking".

5. Children's Privacy

Cheese Asteroid Eater is designed for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the official support channels provided in the App Store.
